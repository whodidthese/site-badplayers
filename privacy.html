Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Badplayers ("the App") respects and protects the privacy of all users. To ensure you feel secure using our services, we provide this Privacy Policy:

1. No Data Collection

The App is a fully offline media player. We do not collect, process, or use any personally identifiable information (such as names, addresses, emails) or usage data.

2. File Access Permissions

To provide playback functionality, the App accesses specific audio files on your device only when you explicitly perform an action (such as clicking the import button). These files are stored strictly within the local sandbox of your device and are never uploaded to any server.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub Issues.
